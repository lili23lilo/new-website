Info. About Us



Fastinfo Class is an online platform which enables you to become an effective communicator in all aspects of life (personal and professional) by providing impressive communication skill classes.

FastInfo Class is an online educational platform where you can have Live Tutorial Classes and guidance from experienced teachers. With us, the students are able to get live teaching classes using audio-visual mode & are also able to communicate with the teachers during the ongoing sessions for any queries or doubt-clearance.

FastInfo Class provides both one-to-one and group classes according to your convenience. We offer essential online spoken English classes like communicative English and business communication.
