We have this fantastic book by Mark Haddon to kick off our list of the top books to improve your English.

The story of this book follows the exploits of Christopher Boone, a 15-year-old boy with high-functioning autism, as he embarks on a difficult journey to look into the killing of his neighbour's dog.
